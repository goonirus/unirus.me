# Terraforming Mars: Venus Next Expansion Rating Draft

Status: public bilingual page drafted at `public/rating/boardgame/expansions/terraforming-mars/venus-next.html`.

## Provisional Review Scope

This draft evaluates the original 2017 `Venus Next` expansion. Later promotional cards and cross-expansion content such as `Prelude 2` are not automatically included.

The official box introduces:

- the Venus board and Venus scale, a fourth global parameter that grants TR but is not a multiplayer end-game condition;
- 49 project cards and 5 corporations;
- the Venus tag, floater resources, a Hoverlord Milestone, and a Venuphile Award;
- the Solar Phase and World Government Terraforming.

These facts define the review object; they do not require every component to receive equal space in the article.

## Confirmed User Judgments

> 主要增加的是玩法上的新路线，金星扩多了大量的金星牌以及全新的模型，机制和联动。然后才是它对于发育党提供了特殊的获取改造度渠道，以及非常吸引人的“金星动物”。但是最有意思的设计还并非这些，而是提供了全球政府的机制，不仅加快了游戏的进程，也让轨道奖励的竞争变的更加激烈。

> 殖民地扩跟本体的联系比金星扩更加紧密。金星扩给人的感觉是在本体之上外挂了一个内容，而殖民地扩更多的是增加了一个行动。

Related accepted judgment from the base-game discussion:

> 一般改造金星的都是不推终局参数的拖拖党，推金星给草本身就很矛盾。

This last point was used to explain why Aphrodite's plant production can work against the usual incentives of a Venus-focused engine. It is a concrete corporation example, not yet a complete judgment of the expansion's balance.

> 我觉得丰富的程度大于扩展带来的臃肿，毕竟只是多一条轨道。可以对比的是民间的月球扩，多了一整个可以改造的星球，就非常臃肿了。

> 其实外挂感的最大原因是加入的牌太弱了，在外挂的同时还起到了污染牌库的作用，这也是“外挂”后最大的扣分点。其实长期来看，起码漂浮物的机制还是很有意思的，天空霸主里程碑的引入也非常正面。

> 主要不推荐新人加的原因还是因为增加的学习成本还是不少的，对新人而言。而对于老手来说，丰富更加重要。我觉得倒也不是有什么原因要拿掉金星吧，主要是新手局加不进去。

> 全扩的时候金星和云牌看似挺合适的，其实很大一部分是后续加得好。在《金星扩展》里的牌，或许是高估了这两者的模型，有一半的牌都是垃圾。

## Already Resolved

- `Venus Next` is not merely another source of TR. Its main contribution is a new family of cards, numerical models, mechanisms, synergies, and strategic routes.
- The TR route for engine-focused players and the appealing Venus-animal cards are meaningful benefits, but they are not the expansion's most important design contribution.
- World Government Terraforming is currently the strongest individual mechanism in the expansion. It advances the game while making global-parameter threshold rewards more contested.
- The expansion's added richness outweighs its extra bulk. One additional track does not by itself make the game bloated.
- The largest practical cost behind the attached-module feeling is the weakness of many added cards. They can feel external to the base engine while also diluting the deck with unattractive draws.
- The apparent strength of Venus-tag and floater cards in a full-expansion environment cannot be credited backward to the original box. Much of that support came from stronger later cards. The user's play-based judgment is that the 2017 expansion overestimated both models and that close to half its cards are effectively junk in most games.
- This weakness does not make every new mechanism unsuccessful. Floaters remain interesting over long play, and the Hoverlord Milestone is a clearly positive addition.
- The fan-made `The Moon` is a useful direct comparison for bloat: it adds a Moon board, three development tracks, three tile families, and a much larger parallel construction system. Use the comparison only to calibrate system size, not to begin a review of that expansion.
- New players face meaningful additional learning cost. Experienced players receive enough extra variety that there is normally little reason to remove Venus after learning it.
- The article should explain both the new route and this integration limit; neither should be erased to force a uniformly positive or negative verdict.

## Confirmed Tier And Recommendation

- Quality tier: `B+ / 有得有失，档内偏上` (confirmed under the revised framework).
- Modifier reason: the expansion's new routes, floaters, Hoverlord Milestone, and World Government Terraforming create a clear net gain, so it sits above a neutral or evenly mixed B. It does not reach A because the original card model, weak-card density, deck pollution, and attached-module feeling materially limit the expansion's overall achievement. Veteran enable frequency is not evidence for the modifier.
- Enable recommendation: `通常加入` (confirmed). It is mainly excluded from teaching games, then normally retained by experienced groups.

Quality reason: the expansion's variety, new routes, floaters, Hoverlord Milestone, and World Government Terraforming demonstrate real design strengths. The principal deduction is not track count but weak card design: the new family can sit outside the strongest parts of the base engine while polluting the deck with unattractive cards. Later cross-expansion support softens this problem in a full pool but does not retroactively improve the original 2017 card model.

Enable reason: the additional learning cost is meaningful for new players. Once the group understands the base game, added variety becomes more valuable and there is normally little reason to remove Venus. This supports `通常加入` but contributes nothing to the letter modifier.

## Current Answer Audit

- Route completion: Codex proposed that Venus is a real but opportunistic strategic family rather than a route players can reliably force. The user has not challenged this answer, but it should remain reviewable before public drafting.
- Ending incentives: the extra route enriches the game more than the extra track bloats it. World Government Terraforming keeps the public game moving and makes threshold rewards more contested; do not reframe the whole expansion as a game-length problem.
- Integration: weak added cards and the resulting deck pollution are the largest deductions. Do not replace this with a generic claim that all Venus mechanisms are self-contained.
- Enable boundary: meaningful learning cost keeps Venus out of teaching games. Once players are experienced, added variety matters more and there is normally no reason to remove it.
- Tier calibration: `B+` is confirmed under the revised `S / A / B / C / F` meanings. Do not reopen it merely because later cards make the full-expansion Venus and floater families look healthier; the review object remains the original box.
