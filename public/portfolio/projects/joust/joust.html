Introduction 游戏简介

Joust is a social bluffing game about knights competing in a grand tournament to win the crowd’s hearts. Each player holds a pool of heart-shaped tokens that represent both their stamina in the tourney and the affection they command from the audience.

On your turn, you challenge another knight to a duel by secretly committing “Courage” into a blind bag. The rest of the table cannot stay neutral: every bystander must send their own hearts as Adoration to one of the two combatants. After the reveal, hearts flow toward the winner — unless the duel is so spectacular that the crowd splits their love evenly between both.

《Joust：比武夺心》 是一款围绕“读人”和押注展开的社交诈唬游戏。 每位玩家都是参加大会武的骑士，手中的心形 token 既代表你的体力与资金， 也代表观众对你的倾慕与支持。

在你的回合，你会向另一名骑士发起比武挑战，将若干“勇气”投入盲袋； 其余旁观的骑士无法置身事外，必须把自己的心 token 作为“芳心”，押注给其中一位参战骑士。 当勇气被公开时，胜利者会赢得战利品与芳心——除非这场对决精彩到让观众决定 把芳心平均献给双方。

Components & Setup 组件与游戏配置

Components

  • 1 shared jousting arena board
  • Heart tokens (recommended 120)
  • 2 “Courage” blind bags

Setup

  • Each player takes 15 heart tokens as their starting pool.
  • Place the arena board in the centre of the table. Put the two “Courage” bags nearby.
  • All remaining hearts form a general supply next to the board.
  • Choose the starting player as the knight “most skilled at charming or deceiving”, or at random if nobody dares to claim the title.

组件

  • 1 个公共的比武竞技场面板
  • 若干心形 token（建议 120 颗）
  • 2 个“勇气”盲抽袋

准备阶段

  • 每位玩家获得 15 颗“心” 作为起始筹码。
  • 将比武面板放在桌子中央，把 2 个“勇气”盲袋放在一旁备用。
  • 其余所有心 token 作为公共供应放在面板旁边。
  • 起始玩家为自认为“最会迷惑人心或最善于骗人”的骑士；若人人谦虚，则随机决定。

Turn Structure 回合流程

On your turn, you must challenge exactly one other player to a joust. The two of you step into the arena, while all remaining players become the audience, ready to offer their hearts.

  1. Secretly commit Courage
    You and your chosen opponent each secretly place any number of your own heart tokens into a “Courage” bag. These hearts represent how far you are willing to go in this duel.
  2. The crowd sends their Adoration
    Every other player must choose one of the two duelling knights and place any number of their hearts in front of that knight as Adoration. You cannot split Adoration in a single duel: all hearts must support exactly one combatant.
  3. Reveal Courage & determine the victor
    Open the bag and compare how many hearts each knight committed as Courage. The knight who committed more hearts wins the joust.
  4. Collect the hearts
    Combine:
    • All Courage hearts from both knights, and
    • All Adoration hearts bet on the losing knight.
    This pile forms the spoils of the duel.
  5. Reward your fans
    For each player who bet their Adoration on the winning knight, pay them a share from the spoils: they receive ⌊ total spoils ÷ number of supporters ⌋ hearts (at least 1), but never more than twice the number of hearts they used as Adoration. The winning knight keeps all remaining hearts.

在你的回合，你必须向一名其他玩家发起一场比武。你们两人走入比武场， 其余玩家则化身观众，将自己的“芳心”押在支持的骑士身上。

  1. 投入“勇气”
    你与被挑战的骑士各自秘密地从自己的心 token 中，拿出任意数量放入“勇气”盲袋。 这些“心”代表你在本场比武中愿意付出的程度。
  2. 观众献上芳心
    其余每位玩家必须在两名骑士中选择一位，将任意数量的心 token 作为“芳心”放在该骑士面前，以示支持。 在同一场比武中，观众不能分摊芳心：所有心必须押给同一位骑士。
  3. 揭晓勇气，判定胜负
    打开“勇气”盲袋，比较两名骑士投入的心数量。 投入“勇气”更多的一方赢得本场比武。
  4. 收集战利品
    将以下 token 汇总在一起，形成战利品堆：
    • 两名骑士投入的所有勇气之心；
    • 所有押注失败那一方骑士的芳心。
  5. 回馈支持者
    对于每位押中获胜骑士的观众： 从战利品堆中给他分发 ⌊ 战利品总数 ÷ 支持者人数 ⌋ 颗心， 向下取整且至少为 1，但不得超过该玩家押注芳心数量的两倍。 获胜骑士获得战利品中剩余的所有心。

Endgame & Ranking 结束与计分

If at any moment a player has no hearts left, they are exhausted and leave the tournament: that player is eliminated from the game.

Play continues clockwise until the table has completed two full rounds of turns (or as close as possible if players are eliminated mid-way). At the end of the second round, each remaining knight counts the hearts they still hold. The player with the most hearts wins the tourney. Ties are shared honours, reflecting a duel where the crowd’s love is perfectly balanced.

In a rare situation where a duel ends in an exact tie of Courage, the joust is declared breathtakingly even: both duelling knights evenly split all hearts involved in that duel (Courage and Adoration together). Any leftover hearts that cannot be split evenly are returned to the general supply.

若在任意时刻，某名玩家的心 token 被耗尽，则视为精疲力竭、无力再战， 立即离开比武场——该玩家直接出局。

游戏顺时针继续进行，直到桌面完成两大轮回合 （若有人中途出局，则以当下最接近“两轮”的状态为准）。 第二轮结束时，仍在场的骑士统计自己剩余的心数量， 拥有最多“心”的玩家赢得大会武的桂冠。 若出现并列，则视为共享荣誉——观众把同等的芳心献给了多位骑士。

若某场比武中，两名骑士投入的“勇气”完全相同，则这场对决被视为精彩绝伦的平局： 两名骑士平均分配本场比武中所有参与的“心” （包括勇气与芳心）。若无法完全平分，多余的心放回公共供应，象征献给整个比赛的掌声。

Optional Variants 可选变体

The following variants are optional modules you can mix and match. Add them only if they fit your group’s personality and how intense you want the tournament to feel.

下列变体规则都是可选模块，可加可不加。 你可以根据桌上玩家的性格、以及希望大会武有多“上头”，自由选择是否加入或组合使用。

Variant 1 – White Heat 变体一：白热化

From the beginning of the second round onward, any knight who enters a joust must commit at least 3 hearts as Courage into the bag. The crowd is fired up; half-hearted duels are no longer acceptable.

从第二轮开始，所有参加比武的骑士，投入“勇气”盲袋的心数量必须至少为 3。 比赛愈发白热化，观众情绪高涨，骑士已经无法再“假装出拳”。

Variant 2 – Riding High 变体二：春风得意

Whenever the knight who currently has the most hearts takes part in a joust, their minimum Courage requirement increases by +1 heart compared to normal. In practice, they can’t play it too safe when they are clearly ahead.

当“场上拥有最多心”的骑士参与比武时， 其在本场比武中投入“勇气”的最低数量提高 1。 换句话说，当你春风得意、心最多时，就不能再轻松划水——你必须比平时投入更多一点的勇气。

Variant 3 – Knightly Conduct 变体三：骑士风度

In every joust, the number of hearts a knight may commit as Courage cannot exceed the total number of hearts their opponent currently owns. You are not allowed to massively overcommit just to crush a weakened rival.

在每一场比武中，任一骑士投入“勇气”盲袋的心数量， 不得超过对手目前持有的心总数。 换句话说，当对手已经身心俱疲时，你不能再靠疯狂砸心来“羞辱对手”， 必须保留一点作为绅士骑士的风度。

Design Notes 设计思路

Joust pushes players into constant social reading: you are never just watching, because every duel forces you to spend hearts as Adoration. The “Courage” bag creates a clean, binary comparison that is easy to resolve but hard to predict, while the payout cap prevents a single lucky bet from completely breaking the economy.

Re-theming money into hearts reinforces the idea that you are gambling not only with resources but also with reputation and affection. The tournament ends quickly—just two rounds—so players are encouraged to make bold, characterful moves rather than hoarding until the very end.

在 《Joust：比武夺心》 中，玩家几乎没有“吃瓜旁观”的空间： 每一场比武都强制所有人投入芳心，迫使你不断判断谁在虚张声势、谁在孤注一掷。 “勇气”盲袋提供的是一个非常干脆的比较机制——结算简单，但过程高度不可预知， 同时通过收益上限，避免单次幸运押注直接摧毁整体经济结构。

将钱币改为“心”，强化了主题：你押上的不仅是资源，也是声望与爱慕。 游戏只进行两大轮，比武节奏较快，鼓励玩家做出符合角色的大胆决定， 而不是一味屯心到最后再出手。
