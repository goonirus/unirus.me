Introduction 游戏简介

Ionized is a 3–6 player abstract game about pushing your personal grid of charged tiles as close to neutral as possible. Each player manages a 3×3 personal board with hidden number tiles and three directional sign tokens that determine how their rows and columns contribute to a final charge.

On your turn, you carefully reveal, swap, and manipulate numbered tiles from a shared bag and a public display. Colour effects let you flip your own tiles, steal or trade with others, or even rotate the plus/minus directions that define everyone’s equations. When one player has fully revealed their grid, the table enters a tense final round before everyone calculates their net charge. The player whose total score is closest to zero wins.

《Ionized》 是一款 3–6 人的抽象桌游。 每位玩家拥有一块 3×3 的个人面板，上面摆放着隐藏数字板块，以及 3 枚带方向的正负号 token。 这些符号会指示某一行或某一列的总和被视为正电荷还是负电荷。

在游戏过程中，你会不断与公共区域的数字板块进行交换，或从盲抽袋中抽取新的数字。 不同颜色的板块带来不同效果：绿色让你翻开自己的格子，红色让你与他人交换数字， 蓝色则可以翻转某位玩家的符号 token，让他们的行列方向“反号”。 当有人率先将自己 3×3 网格中的数字全部翻开时，游戏进入紧张的最终轮： 其他玩家各再行动一次，然后所有人结算电荷。总分最接近 0 的玩家获胜。

Components & Setup 组件与游戏配置

Components

  • 6 double-layer personal boards with a 3×3 grid of slots
  • 24 two-sided sign tokens (each side shows + or -, with an arrow ↔ or ↕ in the lower-right corner)
  • 96 square number tiles that fit snugly into the grids:
    • 48 yellow tiles
    • 24 red tiles
    • 24 blue tiles
    • Numbers 1–12 distributed evenly across all colours
  • 1 cloth blind draw bag for number tiles

Setup

  • Give each player one personal board.
  • Randomly draw three sign tokens and place them on your board in: top-left, centre, and bottom-right. Place them with a random side face up (showing + or - and either ↔ or ↕).
  • Once one player has finished placing their three sign tokens, all other players copy the same three positions on their own boards (but still use their own randomly drawn sign tokens).
  • Each player then randomly draws six number tiles from the bag without looking at them, and places them face down in the remaining six slots of their 3×3 grid.
  • Put all remaining number tiles into the blind draw bag.
  • Draw one tile from the bag and place it face up in the centre of the table; this is the initial public tile.
  • The player who is “worst at arithmetic” becomes the starting player (or choose randomly if no one wants to admit it).

组件

  • 6 个玩家个人面板，每个面板为 3×3 网格（可嵌入板块）
  • 24 枚符号 token，双面印有 + / -， 右下角刻有方向箭头 ↔ 或 ↕
  • 96 个数字板块，大小刚好嵌入格子中，平整无立体手感：
    • 绿色 48 个
    • 红色 24 个
    • 蓝色 24 个
    • 数字 1–12 均匀分布在所有颜色上
  • 1 个用于数字板块的盲抽袋

准备阶段

  • 每位玩家获得 1 个 3×3 的个人面板。
  • 由一名玩家开始，从符号 token 堆中随机抽取 3 个， 随机摆放在自己面板的左上、正中、右下三个槽位， 每个 token 随机选择一面朝上（显示 + 或 - 以及 ↔ / ↕）。
  • 其他玩家按照该玩家的布局，在自己的面板上也将 3 枚符号 token 摆放在相同位置（左上、正中、右下），但使用自己随机抽到的 token。
  • 接着，每位玩家从盲抽袋中随机抽取 6 个数字板块， 不得偷看数字，将其背面朝上放入个人面板剩下的 6 个格子中。
  • 将其余所有数字板块放回盲抽袋。
  • 从盲抽袋中再抽取1 个数字板块正面朝上放在桌面中央， 作为初始公共板块。
  • 自认算术最差的玩家成为起始玩家； 若无人承认，则随机决定。

Turn Structure 回合流程

On your turn, you are always manipulating exactly one number tile: either by swapping with the public tile or drawing a new tile from the bag and deciding what to do with it.

  1. Option A – Swap with the public tile
    Choose one number tile on your personal board (face up or face down). Swap it with the current public tile:
    • The tile you take from the public area is placed face up into your chosen slot.
    • The tile you removed from your board becomes the new public tile, placed face up.
  2. Option B – Draw from the bag
    Draw one new number tile from the blind bag and reveal it immediately. Then choose one of the following:
    • B1: Swap with your grid – Replace one tile on your personal board with the drawn tile (the drawn tile enters your grid face up). The tile you removed becomes the new public tile.
    • B2: Discard for its colour effect – Place the drawn tile face up in the public area, replacing the previous public tile, and resolve its effect:
      • yellow: Flip one of your own tiles face up.
      • Red: Choose one number tile on your board and swap it with a tile on another player’s board (you may swap face down with face up).
      • Blue: Choose one sign token belonging to any player and flip it to its other side, reversing both its sign (+/-) and direction (↔/↕).

After resolving your action, the next player clockwise takes their turn.

在你的回合里，你总是围绕1 个数字板块做出决策： 要么与公共板块交换，要么从盲抽袋抽取新板块并决定如何处理。

  1. 选项 A —— 与公共板块交换
    选择你个人面板上的任意 1 个数字板块（可正面也可背面朝上）， 将其与当前的公共板块进行交换：
    • 你从公共区域拿走的板块以正面朝上的形式放入该格子。
    • 你从自己面板上拿走的板块，则以正面朝上的形式成为新的公共板块。
  2. 选项 B —— 从盲抽袋抽牌
    从盲抽袋中抽取 1 个新的数字板块并立刻翻开，然后二选一：
    • B1：将其换入自己的格子 —— 用抽到的数字板块替换你个人面板上的 1 个格子（新板块以正面朝上放入）， 被替换出来的板块正面朝上放到公共区域，成为新的公共板块。
    • B2：弃置并执行颜色效果 —— 将抽到的板块正面朝上丢入公共区域，取代原有的公共板块， 然后根据其颜色执行对应效果：
      • 绿色：翻开你自己面板上的任意 1 个数字板块。
      • 红色：选择你面板上的 1 个数字板块，与另一名玩家面板上的 1 个数字板块进行交换（可以用背面交换正面的）。
      • 蓝色：选择任意一名玩家的一枚符号 token，将其翻面——符号 +/- 与方向 ↔/↕ 一并反转。

当你完成行动后，顺时针的下一位玩家开始他的回合。

Endgame & Scoring 结束与计分

The moment any player has all nine number tiles face up on their personal board, they immediately trigger the final round.

  • The triggering player does not take any extra turns; their grid is now locked.
  • Each other player gets exactly one more turn following normal rules.
  • During the final round, no action may target or modify the grid or sign tokens of the player who triggered the endgame.

Scoring

Once the final round ends, everyone flips any remaining face-down number tiles face up. Then, each player computes their final score based on their three sign tokens:

  1. For each sign token, look at its arrow:
    • ↔ means the sign applies to the entire row where the token sits.
    • ↕ means the sign applies to the entire column where the token sits.
  2. Sum all numbers in the indicated row or column for that token.
  3. If the token shows +, add that sum to your score; if it shows -, subtract that sum from your score.
  4. Do this for all three sign tokens, then add the three results together to obtain your final score.

The winner is the player whose final score is closest to 0 (ties are shared victories). A positive score represents surplus positive charge, while a negative score means excess negative charge. True mastery lies in carefully steering your chaotic grid towards perfect neutrality.

当任意一名玩家的个人面板上9 个数字板块全部被翻开时， 游戏立刻进入最终轮。

  • 触发结束的玩家不会获得额外回合，他的面板从此视为锁定。
  • 其余每位玩家按照顺时针顺序各进行最后 1 次行动。
  • 在最终轮中，任何效果都不得再影响触发结束玩家的数字板块或符号 token。

计分方式

最终轮结束后，所有玩家将自己剩余的背面板块全部翻为正面。 然后根据 3 枚符号 token 来计算最终电荷：

  1. 对每一枚符号 token，先查看其方向箭头：
    • ↔ 表示该 token 作用于其所在整行的数字之和。
    • ↕ 表示该 token 作用于其所在整列的数字之和。
  2. 将对应行或列上的所有数字相加，得到该行/列的总和。
  3. 若该 token 为 +，则将该总和加到你的分数中； 若为 -，则将该总和从你的分数中减去。
  4. 三个符号 token 分别计算后，将三个结果相加，得到你的最终总分。

所有玩家中，最终总分最接近 0 的玩家获胜（平局则共享胜利）。 正分代表正电荷过剩，负分代表负电荷过剩； 真正的高手会在不断的交换与干扰中，把自己的电荷精细地“调零”。

Optional Variants 可选变体

Variant 1 – Static Shield 变体一：静电护盾

Each player starts the game with a one-time shield token. Before any effect that would flip or move their sign tokens or number tiles (such as a red or blue effect) is applied to them, they may discard their shield to ignore that effect completely. Once used, the shield is gone for the rest of the game.

每位玩家在游戏开始时获得 1 枚一次性的“护盾标记”。 当有任意效果即将改变自己的数字板块或符号 token（例如红色交换、蓝色翻转）时， 该玩家可以弃掉护盾标记，令这次对自己的效果完全无效。 护盾一旦使用就不会再回来，可以选择关键时刻用来保住自己精心调好的电荷格局。

Design Notes 设计思路

Ionized is built around the tension between local optimisation on your 3×3 grid and global disruption through colour effects. Because all number tiles are physically identical on the back, you cannot rely on tactile cues: information must be earned through flipping and cleverly timed swaps.

The row/column sign tokens turn a simple sum puzzle into a small system of equations: you are not trying to maximize or minimize, but to land as close to zero as possible. Effects that tamper with those signs feel dramatic, because they can invert the value of an entire line of numbers in one move.

Finally, the endgame trigger—one player fully revealed—creates asymmetry in tempo. You can rush to force a final round while your grid looks promising, or slow-play to extract more information and manipulate opponents’ charges. The design goal is a quick, thinky experience where the “aha” moment comes from seeing how a small local swap propagates through your entire charge equation.

《Ionized》 的核心是在一个小小的 3×3 网格上， 同时处理局部优化与来自颜色效果的全局干扰。 所有数字板块的背面在手感上完全一致，玩家无法通过摸牌来作弊， 只能通过翻开与交换一点点获取信息。

三枚符号 token 则把简单的“数字求和”变成了一组小型“方程组”： 你既不是单纯做最大化或最小化，而是努力让最终结果尽量靠近 0。 蓝色效果对符号的翻转，往往会一瞬间颠倒整行或整列的正负， 让原本“完美”的布局瞬间变成灾难，也让玩家在交互时更有戏剧张力。

触发结束的条件——“有人率先翻开 9 格”——带来了节奏上的不对称： 你可以选择加速游戏，以自己的布局为中心强行进入最终轮， 也可以选择放缓节奏，多翻一些信息、更多干预别人的电荷。 整体设计希望呈现一种短小精悍、思考密度高的体验， 让玩家在一次次小小的交换中，感受到电荷在网格中被“微调到零”的那种满足感。
