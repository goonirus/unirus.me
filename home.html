Deconstructing Design

A habit of the trade: always taking fun apart to see how it works.

Hello, I'm Donglin Yan

I am a Game Designer obsessed with maps, history books, and complex systems.

"A game is a system with rules and goals where players make decisions that can affect what happens, and this process gives them emotional and interactive experiences, providing spiritual value."

My design philosophy bridges the computational depth of Grand Strategy games and the elegant clarity of modern Board Games.
